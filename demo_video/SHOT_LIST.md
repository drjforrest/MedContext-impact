## Export Settings

**Final Video**:
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- Frame Rate: 30fps or 60fps
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Audio: 48kHz, stereo
- Subtitles: SRT file for accessibility

**Platform Versions**:
- YouTube: 1080p
- LinkedIn: 720p (optional downscale)
- Twitter: 720p with captions embedded
- Mobile: 1080p (vertical crop optional)
